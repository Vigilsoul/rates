Listening

Before you listen.

Analyze the following case:

A change in the exchange rate will make a currency stronger or weaker against other currencies. How will this affect the rest of the economy? Classify the following as effects of a strong currency or effects of a weak currency.

  •  economy grows
  • economy shrinks
  • exports fall
  • exports grow
  • imports are cheaper
  • rate of inflation falls
  • rate of inflation increases
  • workers demand higher wages

Now, Listen and check your answers.

After you listen.

Discuss these questions with a classmate.

  • Do you think we could have a currency for the whole world? Why? Why not?
  • What would it be like to live in a closed economy?
  • What innovations could be considered for exchange rates?
